Privacy Policy for Triset

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Triset is a single-player card game played against computer opponents. This policy explains what information the app handles.

No data collection

Triset does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services, and it does not require an account or internet connection to play.

What's stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of information locally on your device only, using standard iOS app storage. This never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else:

  • The player name you choose to enter (used only to personalize the game screen)
  • Your in-progress game, so you can resume where you left off
  • Your selected color theme preferences

Children's privacy

Because Triset collects no personal information at all, it does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, an updated version will be posted on this page.
